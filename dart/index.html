What's Dart?

Dart allows you to travel more spontaniously. Not sure where to go, but just know you want to go somewhere for a few days, or even a week or two? Dart will find you some random places to experience!

How it works

Dart asks you a few questions on the next page, and with that information, you will be given a list of results fitting your parameters, and listed by price, not destination. Pick somewhere you've never been, and let the adventure begin!
